# Conjuga

If French is the first foreign language you have studied, the term "conjugation" may be new to you. Conjugation is crucial in French because verbs change their form to match the subject of the sentence. For most verbs, you could use six different forms depending on the subject; the verb is paired with subject pronouns are required in French even if the form of the verb sometimes tells who or what you are talking about - so it is essential to conjugate your verbs correctly.

"Conjugation app" can help you achieve that quickly.
